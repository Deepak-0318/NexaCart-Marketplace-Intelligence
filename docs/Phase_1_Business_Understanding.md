# Phase 1 – Business Understanding

## Objective

The objective of this phase was to understand the business problem, identify stakeholder expectations, define the project scope, and establish the business objectives before beginning any technical analysis.

The focus of this phase was not on the dataset, but on understanding the business context and the problem that the analysis aims to solve.

---

# Business Background

NexaCart is a rapidly growing e-commerce marketplace connecting customers and sellers across multiple regions. Although order volume continues to increase, customer satisfaction remains inconsistent due to operational challenges.

The executive leadership has observed issues in delivery performance, seller quality, and customer experience but lacks visibility into the primary factor affecting marketplace performance.

To address these challenges, a comprehensive Business Intelligence audit is required to analyze the marketplace and provide data-driven recommendations.

---

# Problem Statement

Despite increasing order volumes, NexaCart experiences inconsistent customer satisfaction, varying delivery performance, and uneven seller quality.

Leadership is unable to determine whether the primary operational bottleneck lies within seller performance, delivery operations, or customer experience.

The objective of this project is to identify the root cause using historical marketplace data and recommend one strategic improvement supported by evidence.

---

# Business Goal

The primary business goal is to perform a comprehensive Business Intelligence audit and identify the single most impactful improvement that will:

- Improve customer satisfaction
- Reduce order-related issues
- Improve operational efficiency
- Support long-term marketplace growth

---

# Business Objectives

The analysis focuses on the following areas:

- Sales and Revenue Performance
- Seller Performance
- Customer Experience
- Logistics and Delivery Efficiency
- Geography and Regional Insights

---

# Key Business Question

The entire project revolves around answering the following executive question:

> If NexaCart can invest in only one major improvement—Seller Performance, Delivery Operations, or Customer Experience—which investment will produce the greatest improvement in customer satisfaction while reducing order-related issues?

---

# Expected Deliverables

The final deliverables include:

- Interactive Business Intelligence Dashboard
- Comprehensive Marketplace Analysis
- Root Cause Analysis
- Executive Recommendation
- Data-backed Strategic Improvement

---

# Success Criteria

The project will be considered successful if it:

- Identifies the primary marketplace bottleneck.
- Supports findings with data.
- Recommends one strategic improvement.
- Demonstrates measurable business impact.

---

# Outcome

At the end of this phase, the business problem, project scope, objectives, stakeholders, and expected outcomes were clearly defined, providing a strong foundation for the subsequent analytical phases.
